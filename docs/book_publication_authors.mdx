title: Book Publication Authors
description: The `book_publication_authors` table maps authors to book publications. It establishes a many-to-many relationship between users and book publications, specifying the order of authorship.
---

## Table Schema

<TypeTable
  type={{

    },

## Indexes

- **Primary Key**: `(publication_id, author_id)`

## Relationships

- The `publication_id` field references the `book_publications` table.
- The `author_id` field references the `users` table.
