## How to input data ?

- Go to the **Home** page.
- Click the **Input Data** button which can be found below the page title or in the top-right corner of the window.
- Next, select the type of data (like events, students, faculty) you wish to enter. A search bar is also integrated into the page for your convenience.
- You will now be directed to the form containing all the relevant fields. Fill all the fields with the respective information.
- Click on **Submit** button on the bottom right of the form. The form data is successfully submitted.

## Example workflow for inputting data

- To get started, click on the **Input Data** button and select **Events** form.
- Fill in all the fields (event name, data, venue etc.) with the relevant information.
- Submit the form by clicking on the Submit button.
