## How to Input Data

1. Go to the **Home** page.
2. Click the **Input Data** button, which can be found below the page title or in the top-right corner of the window.
3. Select the type of data (like Events, Students, Faculty) you wish to enter. A search bar is also integrated into the page for your convenience.
4. You will be directed to the form containing all the relevant fields. Fill all the fields with the respective information.
5. Click on the **Submit** button on the bottom right of the form. The form data is successfully submitted.

## Example Workflow for Inputting Data

1. To get started, click on the **Input Data** button and select the **Events** form.
2. Fill in all the fields (event name, date, venue, etc.) with the relevant information.
3. Submit the form by clicking on the **Submit** button.
